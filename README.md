<h2 align="left">Hi 👋, I'm Zihong (Logan) Luo</h2>

I am a **Research Assistant** at <a href="https://smartlab.csc.liv.ac.uk/"><strong>SmartLab</strong></a> and a **Computing Science undergraduate** at the University of Liverpool & Xi’an Jiaotong-Liverpool University.

My research lies at the intersection of **multimodal perception** and **robotic action**.  
Moving from medical image analysis toward **Embodied Intelligence**, I am currently building robot agents that can **perceive, reason, and manipulate** in the physical world.

🤖 **Research Interests:** Embodied AI, Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Models, Sim-to-Real Transfer  
🔭 **Current Work:** Gripper-aware VLA policies for adaptive robotic manipulation  
📚 **Selected Publications:** AAAI 2025, ICANN 2024, ICPR 2024
